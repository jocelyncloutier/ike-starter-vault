## Context

A guide to what lands in `+/` and how it gets processed. You can delete this note once you're comfortable with the flow.
## The Inbox Is Where Everything Lands First

Don't overthink where something goes — just capture it in `+/`. AI will help you file it later.
## What Goes Where

**Quick captures** go directly in `+/` root. A rough idea, a thought you want to remember, a link you want to look at later. Create a new note, jot it down, move on.

**Documents** go in `+/Documents/`. PDFs, Word files, spreadsheets — anything you want to reference from your vault. Drop the file here and create a companion note if needed.

**Emails** go in `+/Email/`. Important emails you want to keep in your vault — decisions, approvals, context you'll need later. Copy the relevant content into a note.

**Meeting notes** go in `+/Meetings/`. If you take notes outside your daily note (e.g., a long workshop or a meeting that deserves its own note), put them here.

**Web clips** go in `+/Web/`. Articles, references, and pages captured via the Obsidian Web Clipper browser extension. They arrive as raw markdown and can be formatted later by AI.
## How Items Get Processed

Ask Claude: "Process the inbox" or "File the items in `+/`." Claude will read each item, add proper frontmatter, create links to related notes, and move it to the right location — usually `Atlas/Notes/`, `Atlas/Sources/`, or an existing Effort.

You don't need to process the inbox on a schedule. Do it when it feels full, or ask Claude to do it as part of your morning planning.
