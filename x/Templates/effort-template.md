> [!info] What is an Effort?
> An Effort is something you're actively working on — a project, initiative, or goal. Active efforts live here in `Efforts/`. Move them to `Efforts/Later/` to defer, `Efforts/Paused/` to hold, or `Efforts/Completed/` when done. The folder is the status — no tags needed.

## Context

This is where you describe concisely what this effort is about.
