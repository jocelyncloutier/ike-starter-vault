> [!info] What is a Map note?
> A Map collects links to notes, sources, and external resources about a topic. Name this note "[Topic] Map" (e.g., "Product Strategy Map"). Add sections that make sense for the topic — there's no fixed structure. You can ask AI to generate a Map from your existing notes.
